Welcome to Cyttaria Ragnarok

Cyttaria RO invites you to rediscover the magic of Ragnarok Online with an authentic and challenging experience. On our Low Rates and prerenewal server, without custom items, every detail is carefully thought out to recreate the classic feel of the game while promoting a fair and committed community. From exciting adventures to unforgettable battles, Cyttaria offers a unique environment where your strategy, skill, and friendships will determine your fate.

Embark on an epic journey in Cyttaria RO and leave your mark on the world of Rune-Midgard!
